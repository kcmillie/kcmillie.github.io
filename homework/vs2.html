Comparison: Latitude vs. Humidity

The purpose of this plot was to see if there was any relationships between latitude and humidity. If the value of the latitude is between +20 and +40, humidity varies the most from 10% all the way to 100%. As latitude value moves farther away from this range (20 to 40), humidity varies less. At the farthest points, humidity only ranges from 80 to 100%.
